✦   for you   ✦

you have me, always

— ✦ —

there are some souls
that do not enter a life
as others do.

they do not merely arrive—
they are felt,
as dawn is felt by a sleepless sky,
as warmth is felt by cold hands,
as rain is felt by earth
that had been waiting quietly for mercy.

you feel like that to me.

not like something sudden,
not like something passing,
not like a lovely thing
meant only to be admired from afar—

but like tenderness itself
taking a shape i could love,
and calling it you.

and ever since,
my heart has not known
how to think of you
in any small way.

for you are not small to me.

you are the hush after heaviness.
the comfort after ache.
the softness that remains
when all the noise of the world
has wearied me beyond words.

you are my safest thought.
my gentlest undoing.
my favourite place to rest
without ever having to close my eyes.

and oh,
if only you knew
how beautifully you are held in me.

for it is not only your laughter
that i cherish—
though God knows
your laughter could make a sorrowing day
forget itself.

it is not only your sweetness
when you are light,
nor only your smile
when joy sits easily upon you.

it is all of you.

the happy girl.
the loving girl.
the overthinking girl.
the quiet girl.
the girl with tears gathering first
and words arriving later.
the little storm of you
when anger comes,
and yet even then
you remain somehow so unbearably dear
that my heart can do nothing
but soften.

there is not a single version of you
that i would turn away from.

bring me your chaos.
bring me your silence.
bring me your trembling moods,
your childish moods,
your clingy little heart,
your tired eyes,
your unspoken hurts,
your too-much thoughts,
your every scattered piece—

and i will still look at you
as though i have been trusted
with something precious beyond language.

because that is what you are.

precious
not in the fragile sense,
but in the sacred one.

not because you must be flawless
to be adored,
but because even your imperfect moments
seem touched by something
unusually lovely.

you do not have to come to me polished.
you do not have to arrive glowing.
you do not have to make yourself easier
for me to love you well.

come to me as you are.

come with your laughter falling everywhere.
come with your heart too full.
come with your anger.
come with your tears.
come with your need for comfort
you do not know how to ask for.
come like a child seeking warmth.
come like a tired soul seeking rest.

come,
and let this be the one thing
you never have to doubt:

you have me.

you have me
when the day is golden.
you have me
when the night has been unkind.
you have me
when you are easy to understand,
and when even you do not know
what your own heart is trying to say.
you have me
when you are all smiles and mischief,
and when you are quiet in that way
that makes me wish
i could wrap the whole world in softness
before it reaches you.

you have me
not in the way a thing is claimed,
but in the way a promise is given.

in the way shelter belongs to rain-tired wings.
in the way warmth belongs to winter hands.
in the way home belongs
to the weary heart
that has wandered enough.

that is how you have me.

completely.
gently.
without asking.

and if ever life grows cruel with you,
if ever the world feels too sharp,
if ever your own heart becomes
too heavy for you to carry alone—

come here.

come here with your breaking.
come here with your softness.
come here with the ache you hide so well.
come here with every version of yourself
the world has not known how to hold properly.

i will not ask you to be less.
i will not ask you to be quieter.
i will not ask you to smile first
before i make room for your sorrow.

i will simply open my arms in words
and let them become
the gentlest place i know
to keep you.

because if love has ever meant anything to me,
it has meant this:

to be a resting place
for the one who has become
dearer than rest itself.

and that is what you are.

my princess,
my comfort,
my better half of peace,
my sweetest heart-home,
my favourite person
in all her bright and breaking forms.

you are not loved by me
only in your best moments.

you are loved
in your truest ones.

in the moments you are laughing too hard.
in the moments you are on the verge of tears.
in the moments you are angry and adorable all at once.
in the moments you say nothing at all,
and still your silence reaches me
as though it has known my name forever.

you are loved there too.
especially there.

so let this be the vow
hidden inside every line:

you will never have to earn
the softness i give you.

you already own it.
you already live in it.
you already have a place in me
where no sorrow of yours
will ever be unwelcome.

you have me
like warmth has the flame.
like evening has its prayer.
like a flower has the patient light
that teaches it how to open.
like home has the door
that never minds being knocked upon twice.

you have me, always—
in the loveliest sense,
in the gentlest sense,
in the sense that says:

rest here.
be small here.
be loud here.
be fragile here.
be all that you are
and nothing less.

for i have never wished
to love only the easy parts of you.

i have wished only this—
to be the kind of love
that does not flinch.

the kind that stays.
the kind that soothes.
the kind that gathers your scattered pieces
and kisses them back into belonging,
not with lips,
but with devotion so tender
it begins to resemble grace.

and if one day
you forget everything else i have ever said,
then remember only this:

in every season of you,
in every ache,
in every smile,
in every storm,
in every soft and shining ruin of a day—

you have me.

as comfort.
as promise.
as refuge.
as the quiet hand
still reaching for yours
even after the whole world has gone to sleep.

— your better half veduu, with so much of love
